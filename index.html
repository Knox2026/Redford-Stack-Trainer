User Guide

🎤 Voice Commands

Say clearly: "Five of Clubs" or "Number 14". Green glow means the app is listening.

🧠 Training Modes

N2C: Number to Card
C2N: Card to Number
Next: Card Follows
Prev: Card Precedes
🎧 Bluetooth Fix: If car audio acts like a "Call," go to Bluetooth Settings and toggle OFF "Phone Calls" but keep "Media" ON.

⚠️ Troubleshooting

• Ensure Mic Access is granted in Browser Settings.
• Disable Spatial Audio for all devices.
• Disable Battery Saver for best voice recognition.
