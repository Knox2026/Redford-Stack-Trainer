Stack Trainer Guide

🛠 Interface Legend

  • 🌙/☀️ Theme: Toggle between Light and Dark mode.
  • 📋 Clipboard: View full stack. Tap any card for lifetime stats.
  • 🔥 Fire: Heat Map. (Red = Slow/Wrong, Blue = Mastered).
  • 🏆 Stats: Track current session accuracy and Pace.
  • ⚙ Settings: Adjust card range and activate AI and Voice Modes.
  • 🎤 Mic Icon: Active in Voice Mode. A Green Glow means the app is actively listening.

🧠 Training Modes

Number → Card: Pos to Card
Card → Number: Card to Pos
Random: Swaps N2C and C2N
Next Card: Practice follows
Prev Card: Practice precedes
Before/After: Practice neighbors

⚡ Quick Skip: Correct answers transition in 1.2s. Wrong answers stay for 3.5s to show correction. Tap the card display at any time to instantly skip to the next card.

🤖 What is AI Mode?

AI Mode uses a personalized Spaced Repetition system. The app tracks which cards you struggle with (slow response or wrong answer) and intelligently brings them up more frequently. Mastery reduces card frequency, focusing your time on weak links.

🎤 What is Voice Mode?

Voice Mode allow for a hands-free session. When activated, the app "listens" for your answer. Simply say the card name (e.g., "Five of Hearts") or number (e.g., "Fifteen"). The app validates and automatically proceeds.

📊 Mic Status & Feedback

  • Mic Status: Shows if hardware is standby, active, or blocked.
  • "Heard": Displays exactly what the AI transcribed.

🎧 Bluetooth & Car Audio

If your car/earbuds highjack the mic as a "Phone Call":

Go to Bluetooth Settings > Select device > toggle OFF "Phone Calls" but keep "Media Audio" ON.

🎁 Special Bonus: Card Box Crib

Free card box crib reference with hidden shuffle sequence in UPC code (Si Stebbins to Redford). Sized for Bicycle Poker (2.56" x 3.63").
