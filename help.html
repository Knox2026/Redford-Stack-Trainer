Quick Start Guide

Master the Redford Stack using hands-free voice commands and AI-driven drills.

📲 Step 1: Install as an App

This tool works best when saved to your home screen. This enables full-screen mode and keeps the microphone active.

  • Android (Chrome): Tap ⋮ then "Install App".
  • iPhone (Safari): Tap the Share icon then "Add to Home Screen".

🎤 Step 2: Voice Commands

The AI is trained to recognize specific patterns. For the best results, speak clearly:

  • For Cards: Say "Five of Clubs" or "Jack of Hearts".
  • For Numbers: Say "Number 14" or "Number 5".

Pro Tip: If the app misses a '5' or '7', try saying "Number 5" instead of just "Five".

🔥 Step 3: Heat Map & AI

Tap the Fire Icon to see your progress. Red cards are your "weak spots."

  • Turn AI: ON to have the app automatically repeat your weakest cards more often until you master them.

🏆 Step 4: Tracking Speed

Check the Stats tab. Aim for a "Pace" of under 2.0s. When you hit that consistently, you are ready to perform the stack live!
