Stack Trainer Guide

🛠 Interface Legend

  • 🌙/☀️ Theme: Toggle between Light and Dark mode.
  • 📋 Clipboard: View full stack. Tap any card for lifetime stats.
  • 🔥 Fire: Heat Map. (Red = Needs work, Blue = Mastered).
  • 🏆 Stats: Track current session accuracy and Pace.
  • ⚙ Settings: Adjust card range and toggle AI/Voice modes.
  • 🎤 Mic Icon: Active in Voice Mode. A Green Glow means the app is currently listening for your answer.

🧠 Training Modes

Tap Mode to select your drill:

Number → Card: Say the card at the given position.
Card → Number: Say the stack number for the card shown.
Random: Swaps between N2C and C2N drills.
Next Card: Practice the card that follows the one shown.
Prev Card: Practice the card that precedes the one shown.
Before/After: Practice both neighbors for any card shown.

📊 Mic Status & Feedback

  • Mic Status: Shows if the hardware is standby, active, or blocked.
  • "Heard": Displays exactly what the AI transcribed. Use this to verify if the app is understanding your voice correctly.

🎧 Bluetooth & Car Audio

If your car or earbuds highjack the mic as a "Phone Call":

  • Go to your phone's Bluetooth Settings.
  • Select your device and toggle OFF "Phone Calls" but keep "Media Audio" ON.

⚠️ Troubleshooting & Audio Fixes

  • Browser Permissions: Ensure your default browser (Chrome/Safari) has Microphone Access enabled in your phone's System Settings.
  • Spatial Audio: For 100% audio reliability, Turn OFF Spatial Audio in your phone's sound settings and for all connected Bluetooth devices.
  • Battery Saver: Disable Battery Saver mode; it often lowers audio quality, causing static or recognition errors.
