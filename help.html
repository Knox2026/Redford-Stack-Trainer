Stack Trainer Guide

Master the Redford Stack using hands-free voice commands and AI-driven drills.

📲 1. Installation

This tool is a Progressive Web App (PWA). It works best when "installed" to bypass browser limits:

  • Android: Open in Chrome, tap ⋮ then "Install App".
  • iPhone: Open in Safari, tap Share (square icon) then "Add to Home Screen".

🎤 2. Voice Commands

The AI listens for specific patterns. Speak clearly and naturally:

  • Cards: "Five of Clubs", "Ace of Spades", etc.
  • Numbers: Say "Number 14" or "Number 5".
  • Fuzzy Logic: The app is trained to understand slips like "fine" for 5 or "heaven" for 7.

🎧 3. Bluetooth & Car Audio

If your car or earbuds think the app is a "Phone Call" and highjack the mic:

  • Go to your phone's Bluetooth Settings.
  • Select your device (Car/Earbuds).
  • Toggle OFF "Phone Calls" but keep "Media Audio" ON.

This allows the app to play audio through your speakers while using the phone's built-in microphone for 100% accuracy.

🛠 4. Interface Legend

  • 📋 Clipboard: Full Stack reference (tap a card for lifetime stats).
  • 🔥 Fire: Your Heat Map. Red cards need work; Blue are mastered.
  • ⚙ Settings: Toggle AI Mode, Voice, and set your practice range (e.g., Cards 1-26).

🧠 5. AI Training

When AI: ON is selected, the app stops being random. It will intelligently feed you the cards you struggle with most until you've corrected the "Heat" in your map.
